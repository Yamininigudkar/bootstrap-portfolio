About Artist

Yaminiart started 5 years ago in my Garage.I loved crafting and painting since I was a kid.Over the years I have experimented with various art forms

We bought our own house five years back, like every new house owner I had a specific idea in mind about decorating it.This nudged me into making my own Home decor art pieces.Friends and family loved these pieces and I received a lot of encouragement to enable others in fulfilling their vision of Home Decor

I make various Home decor items like Candle-Holders,Vases,Glass painting on Frames,Canvases, Jars etc. All my work gets its uniqueness because of its combination of "Henna style" designs and Glass painting.

I have participated in various Art and Craft Vendor fairs and received a lot interest and appreciation.
